Payroll Giving

What is Payroll Giving?

Payroll Giving is a charitable giving program that allows employees to contribute a portion of their salary for the social cause of their choice. This contribution is deducted automatically from their paycheck, facilitated by their employer, making it a convenient and efficient way to support charities.

Small, regular donations add up to significant support. Your monthly contributions, no matter the size, ensure a steady stream of assistance to those in need. Payroll Giving provides employees with a convenient, tax benefits, and meaningful way to contribute to charitable causes, aligning their personal values with their workplace and fostering a sense of community and social responsibility
