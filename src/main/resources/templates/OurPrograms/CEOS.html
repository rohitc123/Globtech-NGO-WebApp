Coding Education in Goverment Schools

We are bringing the gift of coding education to the most deserving students—those in grades 8 to 10, hailing from marginalized backgrounds, and attending under-resourced government schools.

Our mission is clear—to bridge the educational gap and empower these young minds with the digital skills they need to thrive in the 21st century.

Join us in equipping these resilient students with the tools they need to break free from the cycle of marginalization and disadvantage in this digital age.

Objective

Through dynamic lessons and hands-on coding experiences, we aim to cultivate not just technical proficiency but also critical thinking and creativity.

We are breaking down barriers to ensure that every student, regardless of their circumstances, has access to a brighter future.
