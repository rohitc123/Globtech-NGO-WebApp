Coding on Wheels

About Coding on Wheels

The "Coding on Wheels" project is a transformative initiative of Global Technopower Foundation (GTPF) dedicated to empowering the most vulnerable and often overlooked members of our society: Destitute students in orphanages.

These children face unique challenges, and our project is committed to bridging the digital divide for them. With a mobile learning unit equipped with cutting-edge technology and expert instructors, we take the world of coding and computer science directly to the doorsteps of orphanages.

By providing interactive coding lessons, workshops, and engaging activities, we introduce these students to the exciting world of technology.

Objective

Our goal is to not only impart essential coding skills to destitute students but also nurture creativity, critical thinking, and a thirst for knowledge, enabling them to chart their own path to a brighter future.

"Coding on Wheels" is a vehicle of change, breaking down barriers, and ensuring that Mumbai's orphanage students have the tools to grow socially and economically, becoming self-reliant and successful individuals in the future.
