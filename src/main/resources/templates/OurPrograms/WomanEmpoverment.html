Women Empoverments

We aim to champion a transformative mission of empowering women and girls to reclaim their lives from the clutches of monthly discomfort and pain. Our intervention is simple yet profound - providing access to Mihir organic rice, a natural remedy with the potential to alleviate menstrual agony.

Beyond mere distribution, we are igniting a movement. We conduct awareness programs, nurturing health and hygiene education, equipping women and girls with the knowledge to manage their menstrual health confidently.

Objective

To break the silence, challenge taboos, and ensure every woman and girl, regardless of her circumstances, stands strong in the face of a monthly challenge.

Join us as we empower these resilient individuals to take control of their well-being, transforming not just their lives but entire communities.
