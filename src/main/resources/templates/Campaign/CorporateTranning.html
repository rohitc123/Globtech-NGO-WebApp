Welcome to " Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi," a trailblazing campaign that seamlessly integrates corporate growth with social responsibility. As we propel your team's skills forward, we collectively aim to uplift the lives of orphanage students and underprivileged children attending government schools through coding education, facilitated by our non-profit organization

How It Works:

Engage your employees in cutting-edge technology training, and in return, we request a minimum donation of 5000 rupees per participant. The accumulated donations will be directed towards providing coding education to orphanage students and underprivileged children in government schools, fostering a brighter and more technologically inclusive future. All the donations that you will provide are eligible for 50% tax exemption benefits under section 80G of Income Tax Act as we are the non-profit organisation.
